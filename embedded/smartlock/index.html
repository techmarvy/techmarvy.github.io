BLE Smart Lock

This project involved design and development of prototypes for a Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE) based Smart Lock. We chose an FCC certified Raytac module with the Nordic nRF52832 SoC as the controller. As per the requirements, we also incorported a motor driver, an accelerometer and RGB LED on the PCB. Deliverable was multiple assembled PCBs with firmware as per client specifications.
