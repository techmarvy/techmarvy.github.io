IoT Gas Sensor

This goal of this project was to design an IoT device that senses LPG gas in typical kitchen or industrial scenario. The form factor of the device was that of a plug, with an OLED display, gas sensor, buzzer and a rechargebale LiFePo4 battery for power backup. For internet connectivity we used a TI CC3200 WiFi SoC.
